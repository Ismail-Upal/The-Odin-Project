Biryani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful South Asian dish made with aromatic basmati rice, marinated meat, and a blend of spices.
It's often layered and cooked slowly to allow all the rich flavors to come together. Popular variants include chicken, beef, mutton, and vegetable biryani.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or mutton (cut into pieces)
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Biryani masala or whole spices
  • Saffron or food coloring (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (mint and coriander)
  • Ghee or oil

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste. Let it rest for at least 1 hour.
  2. Fry sliced onions until golden brown and set aside some for garnishing.
  3. Cook the marinated meat with tomatoes until tender and oil separates.
  4. Boil rice until it's 70% cooked. Drain the water.
  5. In a heavy pot, layer rice and meat alternately. Add fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron on top.
